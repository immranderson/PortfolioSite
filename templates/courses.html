About The Class

I find that a lot of programming classes that have been offered assume that the student already has at least a light background of coding. I set out to create a class targeted towards the population of people that have never coded before!

In this course, I will introduce some key computer science concepts, and show you how to understand their relation to Android and Mobile Development. The second portion of the course will enable students to apply the concepts they have just learned in order to create their own application.

About The Class

People everywhere in the industry use fancy letters and numbers all the time to represent colors! However, a vast majority of them don't really understand why... This course aims to explain exactly what's going on and give the student a firm understanding of the theory of how we use numbers to represent colors in our day to day lives!
