About

EmotionLine was built by myself within a 24-hour period during the 2013 Techcrunch Disrupt San Francisco Hackathon. The idea came about when I found myself pre-occupied with the idea of how there were plenty of services in which you could keep a journal, but how those services did nothing with that data except store it. I figured, wouldn't it be nice if there was an app out there where it would be dead simple to not only record how your day was going, but also use that information to give you ideas on how to improve your life? My ultimate goal with EmotionLine is to reduce the global rate of suicide and spread positivity throughout the world.
